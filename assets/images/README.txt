GENERAL PHOTOGRAPHY TIPS
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Shoot on a sunny day with good natural light
  - Use a wide-angle lens for pools to capture the full space
  - Include context: sky, landscaping, patio furniture for lifestyle feel
  - Take before-and-after shots of every project
  - Get client permission in writing before using their property photos
  - Compress images to WebP format, max 200KB per image for fast load
  - Name files descriptively: "backyard-pool-patio-austin-tx.webp"
    (good for SEO image search)
